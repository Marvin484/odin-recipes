Chocolate Tapioca Porridge

Description

This is a quick meal that can be made and had anytime of the day.

Ingredients

  • Tapioca pearls
  • Condensed milk
  • Chocolate chip
  • Roasted peanut (optional)

Steps

  1. Add the tapioca pearls in a microwavable bowl, add enough water to it so the water is a bit above the tapioca, and let it soak for 15 minutes.
  2. Add a bit more water to it, stir, and put in the microwave for a minute.
  3. Take the bowl out of the microwave, stir the content, and put it back into the microwave for another minute. Watch for overflowing.
  4. Take the bowl out again, stir the content, and if it is too thick, add 4 tablespoons of water to it and stir.
  5. Take the bowl out, and add condensed milk to the tapioca until you get the desired sweetness.
  6. Add the chocolate chips to the tapioca and add roasted peanuts if you want.
