Shrimp Fried Rice

Description

You've had several kinds of fried rice before but this recipe will make you feel like you've never had fried rice before

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 3 table spoons of oil of your choice (preferably coconut oil)
  • Shrimp
  • Green pepper
  • Broccoli
  • Onion
  • 4 eggs
  • Spices (garlic powder, crushed red pepper, cajun spice, ginger powder, and whatever spices you'd like)
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Curry

Steps

  1. Cook the rice for 20 minutes.
  2. While the rice is cooking, chop green pepper, broccoli, onion, and tomatoes.
  3. Add 2 tablespoons to a frying pan and let it warm up on high heat.
  4. Turn the heat level down to medium.
  5. Add green pepper to the oil and leave it on for about 7 minutes, stirring it from time to time.
  6. Add shrimp to the pan and stir it for 3 minutes.
  7. Add the broccoli in.
  8. Add the spices and stir for 2 minutes.
  9. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add tomatoes and onions to the bowl, and beat the eggs with the tomatoes and onions until you get an even mixture
  10. Add salt to the mixture.
  11. Add the egg mixture to the pan and stir until it starts to look scrambled.
  12. Add the rice and curry in and stir for 2 minutes.
  13. Leave it on for 10 minutes, stirring it every 2 minutes.
  14. Let cool down for 5 minutes and serve.
