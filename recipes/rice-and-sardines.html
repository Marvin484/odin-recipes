Rice and Sardines

Description

This is a simple yet delicious meal that doesnt take much to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • Blackeye peas
  • 2 cans of sardines in oil
  • Salt
  • Hot sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Tomato sauce or pasta sauce
  • Spices (garlic powder, crushed red pepper, cajun spice, ginger powder, and whatever spices you'd like)

Steps

  1. Cook the blackeye peas for 30 minutes.
  2. Add the rice in with the blackeye peas and cook for 20 more minutes.
  3. While the rice and beans are cooking, pour the oil from the sardine cans into a frying pan and let it heat up.
  4. Add 3 tablespoons of tomato paste and let it fry until the the color of the paste turns dark.
  5. Turn the heat down to meduim, add in the spices and salt, and stir for 2 minutes.
  6. Add in the tomato or pasta sauce and hot sauce, and let it cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add in the sardines and let it cook for 10 more minutes.
  8. Let it cool down for 5 minutes, and serve with the rice and beans.
